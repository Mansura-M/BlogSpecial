  1. No matter how good you look people will find ways to degrate you so instead of worrying about what others think of you,if you work on yourself you will definitely see some good changes in you.
  2. You have to undertand that insecutity is phase every person in this world goes through at a particular period of their life whether its work,family,beauty standards etc just Remember no one's perfect and beuaty lies in imperfections.

  4. sometimes we all think that lifes a never ending saga of sadness and we try to compare our journey with others but you have to realize that different people live different lives and you wont be able yo live like them because it was never it meant for you.
