How to Lead A Simple Life

When it comes to life society makes it sound so complicated that we often forget how simple and beautiful life is.Living your life doent not have to be grand,eating what you like,doing things you love is also considered living.To lead a simple and peaceful life all you need to do is stop worrying about future.we all know how uncertain future is.No matter how hard we try some things are meant to happen in our life.You should plan for your future but at the same time you should take time to enjoy the present moment.Having a healthy mindset is the key to lead a simple and peaceful life.Now you may question what exactly is a healthy mindset and how does one accquire it?

To a healthy mindset one should stop lying to their own self and start being tru to themselves.There will be times when you might make mistakes and during those times instead of lying to satisfy yourself,one should start accepting the truth even though the truth might hurt you.Being true to oursellves is something we all should do to have a healthy mindset instead of drowing yourself with pure lies that will never satisfy your soul.

Stop comapring yourself with others.Different people have different talents and one cant expect to have them all,so instead of comparing,you can try to find the talents you have within yourself.Always remember comaprison is the destroyer of Happiness

Try to indulge yourself with activities like admiring nature "Nothing gives peace like nature does" if you start noticing the small and pretty things that happens in nature like clouds,rain etc you will definitely see some big changes in yourself.
